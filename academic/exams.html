Final Examination

Cumulative assessment of all course material, making up 50% of your final grade. Conducted under strict supervision.

Evaluation Process

1

Submission

All exam materials are collected and securely stored to maintain integrity.

2

Blind Grading

Exams are graded anonymously to ensure fairness and eliminate bias.

3

Double-Check

Each exam is reviewed by two independent evaluators for accuracy.

4

Moderation

A committee reviews borderline cases and unusual results.

5

Results Compilation

All components are combined according to the weighting system.

6

Publication

Final grades are published through secure channels with appropriate feedback.

Grading Scale

Score Range Grade Points

Below 60% F (Fail) 0.0

Note: Minimum passing grade is D (60%) for most courses
